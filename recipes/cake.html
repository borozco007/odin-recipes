Chocolate Cake

Description

This a recipe for a tasty chocolate cake. Below will be all the ingredients needed to make it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cup of Flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 5 tablespoons of milk
  • 1 cup of chopped pecans

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 11x17 jelly roll pan.
  2. Mix white sugar and flour in a large bowl. Combine 1 cup butter, water, and 3 tablespoons cocoa powder in a small saucepan; stir over medium heat until butter is melted and mixture is well combined.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of cake comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and allow to cool on a wire rack.
  5. Combine remaining 1/2 cup butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa powder, and milk in a small saucepan; stir over medium heat until butter is melted and mixture is well combined.
